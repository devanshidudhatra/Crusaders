About the Project

Problems :

Space radiation poses significant risks to both satellites and astronauts.
High levels of radiation can lead to equipment malfunctions, degraded performance, and potential health hazards.
The challenge is to accurately assess whether specific radiation values, given as wavelength and irradiance, are harmful and to predict their impact.
Radiation can damage DNA, leading to cellular mutations and increased risk of diseases.

Solutions :

Our solution is a predictive model that evaluates whether given values of wavelength and irradiance are harmful to satellites and astronauts.
